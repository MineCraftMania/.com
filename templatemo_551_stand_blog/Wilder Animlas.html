Wilder Animals
  • By TheCartoon
  • Jan 18, 2022

The goal of this resource pack is to add variety to the animals of minecraft.
Version 1.0 features:



-Chicken: New model and 8 texture variants.(including 1 for the baby and 1 for when spawned in Soul Sand Valley)



-Cow: New model and 8 texture variants.



-Pig: New model and 8 texture variants.



-Sheep: New model and texture.



-Goat: 7 texture variants. (3 for colder biomes including the original, 3 for hotter biomes and 1 for the nether)
